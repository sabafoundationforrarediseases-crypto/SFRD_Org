Join Our Mission

This isn't just a job. It's a chance to build a national movement from the ground up and make a lasting impact on millions of lives.

Why Work With Us?

We are a team of passionate, mission-driven individuals dedicated to creating systemic change. At SFRD, you will find a culture of:

Purposeful Impact

Every task, every project, every partnership directly contributes to helping a family in need. Your work has a clear and profound purpose.

Growth & Learning

You'll be at the forefront of building a national ecosystem, working with leaders across healthcare, policy, and technology. The learning curve is steep and rewarding.

Collaborative Spirit

We are a lean, agile team that thrives on collaboration. Every voice is heard, and every idea is valued as we build this foundation together.
